# V2 Roadmap

## Priority ideas after the core product is real

### Tier 1 — Highest leverage
1. Emergency Freeze / Kill Switch
2. Pre-Approved Trusted Destinations
3. Why Approved / Why Blocked explanation layer
4. Permission Presets / Personalities
5. Private Review toggle

### Tier 2 — Strong product depth
6. Permission Timeline
7. Auto-Approve Confidence Ladder
8. Smart Suggestions

### Tier 3 — Strategic leverage
9. Human / Agent / Vendor identity layer
10. Receipt as a first-class object

## Rule
Do not build Tier 2 or Tier 3 before the core product truth is established.
Hashapp must first be:
- non-custodial
- wallet-connected
- permission-real
- receipt-real
- Track-1 credible
